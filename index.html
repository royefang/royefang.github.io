About Me

Hello, my name is Roye Fang and I am currently a second year Computer Engineering (CE) student at UCLA, based in the Greater Los Angeles Area. I am intrigued by software and hardware, and I hope to bring skillsets from both to any of my endeavors. I also have a love for teaching, allowing me to channel my enthusiasm for mentorship. In my spare time, I enjoy cooking, hiking, playing piano, and immersing myself in the marching arts.


Check out some of my projects below, as well as my resume and contact information.

iPoduino

iPoduino is an Arduino-based music player that can shuffle through three songs/pieces.

time.out

time.out is an ESP32 based alarm clock that features Bluetooth control and an interactive user interface.

passwrd

passwrd is a password generator that can hash random phrases into a secure string or produce completely arbitrary passwords.

Mori

Mori is a simple game that tests an individual's short-term memory and mental endurance.

TI-RSLK

The TI-RSLK is a line-following car, programmed in Energia. The car uses IR sensors in a PID feedback algorithm, steering the car on any arbitrary path.
