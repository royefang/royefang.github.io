About me

Hello my name is Roye! Thanks for checking out my page.

I am currently a third year CS major at UCLA. I switched from EE to CS after realizing the sheer breadth of applications that computer science has. Despite switching majors, I still plan to remain active in IEEE and continue applying my knowledge in practical settings.

This past summer (2021), I had the wonderful opportunity to tackle an internship at The Aerospace Corporation. Working for the Visualization and Immersive Technologies Department (VITD), I got exposure to a broad range of technologies, industry experience, and—most importantly to me—people.

I also champion the principles of mentorship, both as a mentor and a mentee. Having been inspired by some wonderful teachers and mentor figures in my own life, I returned that knowledge as a tutor for iD Tech Camps throughout my 2nd year.

Outside of work and school, I love spending time around music, cooking, hiking, and drinking milk. I currently play bass drum for Broken City Percussion.
